Fire Safety Training

When the fire alarm sounds, all employees must stop what they are 
doing and evacuate the building immediately. Do not use elevators 
during an evacuation.

The assembly point is located in the parking lot outside the main 
entrance. All employees must report to their fire warden at the 
assembly point.

Fire wardens are responsible for sweeping their floor to ensure 
everyone has evacuated. Re-entry is only allowed after the fire 
warden gives the all-clear signal.

Never attempt to fight a fire unless you are trained to do so. 
Your safety is the priority.
